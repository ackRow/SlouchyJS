After training the neural network, you will be able to save it using your account so that you won't need to train it again every time you're using SlouchyJS.

Information

SlouchyJS helps you adjust your posture during long hours spent in front of your computer.

Your webcam output will be processed in real-time locally by a neural network (using tensorflowJS) to monitor your posture.

This application has been designed so that no record from your webcam will be transfered through the internet.

Engineered by Hugo Rosenkranz | Contact me at hugo.rosenkranz@gmail.com.

An original idea from pyskell.

How to Use

Since this project is in an early stage of its developement, it only supports Google Chrome.

First of all, you will need to train the network by pressing the Get Started button and record yourself following the instructions below the video.

If the training went well, you can let this tab open while conducting your usual digital activities and SlouchyJS will automatically notify you if you are slouching.
